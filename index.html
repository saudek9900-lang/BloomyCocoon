Our Story

BloomyCocoon is run by sisters from their home in Mampad, Kerala. What started as a cozy afternoon project has blossomed into a shared passion and a dedication to the slow, gentle rhythm of handcrafted crochet.

Working side-by-side from our home, we believe that in an age of instant gratification, there is profound beauty in the slow. Every piece we create is driven by the belief that handmade gifts carry an emotional warmth and a tactile history that mass production can never replicate. When you hold a BloomyCocoon creation, you're holding hours of our focused patience, mutual encouragement, and genuine care.

auto_awesome Orders & Customization

Can I customize colors? expand_more

Absolutely! Every BloomyCocoon piece is made to order. You can choose from our curated palette of Sage Green, Dusty Pink, and Warm Creams, or request a specific shade via WhatsApp. We love matching our creations to your unique space.

Do you accept custom bouquet requests? expand_more

Yes, we specialize in everlasting bouquets. Whether it's for a wedding, anniversary, or home decor, we can design a custom floral arrangement that never wilts. Each petal is individually hand-hooked for a premium, realistic texture.

How does WhatsApp ordering work? expand_more

We believe in a personal touch. When you click "WhatsApp Us", you'll be connected directly with our maker. We'll discuss your preferences, send you yarn swatches, and finalize the payment details securely within the chat.

schedule Crafting & Delivery

How long does crafting take? expand_more

Slow living is at the heart of our craft. Small items take 3-5 days, while larger blankets or custom bouquets may take 2-3 weeks. We’ll provide a precise timeline when you place your order.

wash Product Care

How should crochet products be cared for? expand_more

To maintain the texture, we recommend gentle hand washing in cool water with mild detergent. Lay flat to dry on a clean towel. Avoid hanging, as this can stretch the delicate stitches. Every order includes a detailed Care Guide.
