Our Story

BloomyCocoon is run by sisters from their home in Mampad, Kerala. What started as a cozy afternoon project has blossomed into a shared passion and a dedication to the slow, gentle rhythm of handcrafted crochet.

BloomyCocoon is a handmade crochet gifting brand from Mampad, Malappuram, Kerala, creating soft crochet gifts for customers across Kerala and India.

Working side-by-side from our home, we believe that in an age of instant gratification, there is profound beauty in the slow. Every piece we create is driven by the belief that handmade gifts carry an emotional warmth and a tactile history that mass production can never replicate. When you hold a BloomyCocoon creation, you're holding hours of our focused patience, mutual encouragement, and genuine care.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our slow-crafted crochet treasures. Find answers about custom orders, delivery, and caring for your handmade cocoons.

Crafted with Love

shopping_bag Ordering & WhatsApp

How do I order from BloomyCocoon? expand_more

Browse the collection, add your favourite crochet gifts to cart, and continue to WhatsApp. Our team will confirm customization, final price, delivery and order details personally.

Can I order directly through WhatsApp? expand_more

Yes. The website is designed around WhatsApp ordering so customization, price and delivery can be confirmed personally before your handmade crochet gift is started.

Why are prices shown as starting prices? expand_more

Crochet gifts are handmade and often customized. Final pricing can change based on size, color, design complexity, packaging and delivery.

auto_awesome Custom Orders & Gifts

Do you accept custom crochet orders? expand_more

Yes. We accept custom crochet orders for bouquets, plushies, keychains, baby gifts, accessories, home decor and gift combos based on your idea, colours and occasion.

Can I customize colours and designs? expand_more

Yes. Colours, size, flower type, gift notes and other design details can be discussed and finalized through WhatsApp.

Are crochet bouquets good gifts? expand_more

Yes. Crochet bouquets are long-lasting keepsake gifts that do not wilt, making them suitable for birthdays, anniversaries, Valentine’s Day and special surprises.

Are there baby gift options? expand_more

Yes. BloomyCocoon offers handmade baby gift options suitable for newborn gifting and baby shower surprises, including soft booties, caps, mittens, rattles and baby-themed gift sets.

local_shipping Delivery, Time & Care

Do you deliver across India? expand_more

Yes. BloomyCocoon is based in Mampad, Malappuram, Kerala and delivers handmade crochet gifts across India.

Do you deliver within Kerala? expand_more

Yes. We deliver handmade crochet gifts across Kerala, including Malappuram and nearby areas depending on courier availability.

How long does a handmade crochet order take? expand_more

Timelines depend on the size and customization of the item. Small pieces may take a few days, while larger custom orders can take longer.

How should crochet products be cared for? expand_more

Handle gently. For washable items, use mild detergent, hand wash softly, lay flat to dry and avoid rough handling.
